All About Cats

Why Cats Make Great Pets

Cats are wonderful companions known for their independence and affection. They are perfect pets for both busy households and quiet homes.

Basic Cat Care

  • Fresh water daily
  • Quality cat food
  • Clean litter box
  • Regular vet checkups

Popular Cat Breeds

  • Persian - Known for their long fur and calm personality
  • Siamese - Vocal and social cats with distinctive coloring
  • Maine Coon - Large, friendly cats with thick fur
  • British Shorthair - Easy-going cats with plush coats

Cat Behavior and Body Language

  • Purring - Usually indicates contentment
  • Tail up - Friendly greeting
  • Slow blink - Shows trust and affection
  • Kneading - Sign of comfort and happiness

Fun Facts About Cats

  • Cats spend 70% of their lives sleeping
  • A cat's nose print is unique, just like a human's fingerprint
  • Cats can jump up to six times their length
  • A group of cats is called a clowder
